LiveLedger! - What is it?

LiveLedger! is a online tool that people can use to keep a running ledger tracking how much money that they spend. Income and charges can be added with ease. The most powerful thing about liveledger! is that it is online, and can be shared easily with other LiveLedger! users. LiveLedger! is also totally manual, which allows the user full control of what is on and off of the "books".
